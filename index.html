About Me

I'm ARNHEIT, a 13-year-old cybersecurity enthusiast specializing in ethical hacking and system analysis. My journey began at age 8 when I first discovered the world of penetration testing and reverse engineering.

What started as curiosity has evolved into a deep passion for understanding system vulnerabilities and developing robust security solutions. I believe in using my skills to make the digital world safer.

My Journey
